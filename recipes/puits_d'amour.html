Puits d'amour

Home

Description

An exotic twist on the classic Puits d'amour. Fruity mango revivified by the subtly of Espelette pepper and the freshness of coriander, finished with an enticing coconut chiboust cream and crispy sweet shortcrust pastry.

Ingredients

Sweet shortcrust pastry:

  • 160g butter
  • 200g icing sugar
  • 40g ground almonds
  • 3g fine salt
  • 1g lemon zest
  • 1/2 vanilla pod
  • 263g T55 flour
  • 52g whole eggs

Egg wash:

  • 100g egg yolks
  • 25g single cream

Mango cream:

  • 225g mango purée
  • 225g reduced mango purée
  • 40g egg yolks
  • 1.3g Stab 2000 stabiliser
  • 3.3g 325NH95 Pectin
  • 50g butter

Mango mixture:

  • 10g fresh coriander
  • 250g mango cream
  • 250g finely diced mango
  • 1.5g Espelette pepper

Mango confit:

  • 140g mango purée
  • 140g fresh mango
  • 35g lemon juice
  • 15g yuzu juice
  • 55g caster sugar
  • 10g glucose powder
  • 15g iulin
  • 7.5g 325HN95 Pectin
  • 0.7g xanthan gum

Génoise

  • 225g eggs
  • 165g caster sugar
  • 10g glucose
  • 150g T55 flour
  • 40g butter
  • 60g milk
  • 100g diced ginger
  • 100g mango confit

Coconut chiboust cream:

  • 140g coconut purée
  • 60g milk
  • 0.7g lime zest
  • 10g lime juice
  • 1/2 vanilla pod
  • 5g La Perruche brown sugar
  • 65g egg yolks
  • 13g Maizena
  • 14g prepared gelatine
  • 95g egg whites
  • 75g La Perruche brown sugar
  • 30g water
