Cinnamon Roll

Home

Description

Fluffy viennoiserie made from brioche dough generouly filled with a mix of butter, La Perruche Cassonade and cinnamon, then topped with a creamy glaze.

Ingredients

Brioche:

  • 400g T45 flour
  • 80g warm milk
  • 60g La Perruche Cassonade
  • 60g butter
  • 15g fresh baker's yeast
  • 4g baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla flavoring
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Filling:

  • 50g soft butter
  • 30g La Perruche Cassonade
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon

Icing:

  • 90g La Perruche Cassonade
  • 45g butter
  • 15 cl single cream
  • 1 pinch fleur de sel
